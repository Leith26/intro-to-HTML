About the buttons

Have you heard the kids today talking about "Dabbing"? "Dabbing" is the dance move displayed by the cartoon animals on our buttons.
We wanted our buttons to reach out to kids as much as adults on the subject of spreading love not hate by using the hip trend, "Dabbing. And who doesn't love cute cartoon animals? The buttons have a bright colour scheme, and they are for two great causes.
